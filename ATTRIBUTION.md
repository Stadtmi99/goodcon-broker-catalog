# Broker catalog sources & attribution

The Good Con broker catalog is generated from public sources by `generate.py`.

## justvanish
Opt-out emails and URLs are derived from **justvanish** by AnalogJ
(https://github.com/AnalogJ/justvanish), used under the MIT License.

Future sources (planned): California CPPA and Vermont data broker registries
(public records).

No user personal data is involved in generation; the catalog is public,
non-personalized broker information.
